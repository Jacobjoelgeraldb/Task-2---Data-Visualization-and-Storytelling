# Task 2 - Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Objective
Created an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze Superstore sales data and communicate business insights.

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- Superstore Dataset Cleaned Dataset.csv

## Dashboard Includes
- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Region
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Customer Segment
- Top 10 States by Sales
- Region Slicer

## Business Insights
- Identified the highest-performing product category.
- Compared sales across regions.
- Analyzed customer segments.
- Observed sales trends over time.
- Highlighted top-performing states.
